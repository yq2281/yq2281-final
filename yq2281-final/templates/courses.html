Beginning Poetry Workshop has by far been my favorite class this semester. I love the teacher, I love my classmates, and I love writing poetry. I'm so grateful that I was able to take this class; it was a welcome break from my STEM-heavy course load last semester. I think everyone deserves the chance to see the world through poetry's lens. There's something really beautiful about finding the poetics in everything. I truly believe that through this class, I've learned to become a happier, better person.
